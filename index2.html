ART TECHNICAL ITALY's business activities brief description.

A.T.I. settled through a team of oil and gas experts with the ambition
to offer services in Libyan oil & gas through a commercial network
with companies (mainly Italians) well established in different parts
of the oil and gas business maintenance and construction of,
oil and gas plants (including compressors and turbines) o pipe lines, then:

• Well construction
• Rig and rig less well services
• Geoscience
• Petroleum engineering
• Man Power

First selected partner is a holding, able to cover a large spectrum of activities,
within the frame of mechanical design and construction for:

• Power stations
• oil and gas plants
• equipped for skid/modular process vessels prefabrication.

Their personnel, continuously trained and updated, is skilled to manage the daily activities in accordance with the most stringent safely rules, achieving in the meanwhile Client's timeframe and qualify expectations.

We are cratifed !

Our Certified under the following international standards:

Quality management system: ISO 9001,
Welding activities: lSO 3834-2:2006
Process vessels fabrication: ASME STAMP, codes S, U, U2

Industrial base has around 20.000 m2 uncovered area and 10000 m2 covered workshop areas. Workshops are capable to deal with all type of material: carbon steel, stainless steel, duplex, super-duplex and so on. Separated "white" covered areas are dedicated to the handling of special base materials, SS, duplex, super-duplex.

Almost of the production areas are located in Ravenna commercial Port Area, around 150 meters far away from the loading-in docks, granting the industrial base a strategic position to prefabricate oversized structures and ship them all over the world by sea transportation.
